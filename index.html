ABOUT GREENFOREST

Forest green is a representation of the average color of the leaves of the trees of a temperate zone deciduous forest.Forest green is a representation of the average color.

Green Forest was retired from racing to become a breeding stallion at Jonabell Farm. Despite a promising start to his stud career, he was not a commercial success in USA.By far his most successful offspring was the filly Forest Flower, from his first crop of foals, whose wins included the Irish 1000 Guineas. Other good winners included Made of Gold (Royal Lodge Stakes), Forest Wind (Mill Reef Stakes), and Green Line Express, who won at Group Three level and finished second to Zilzal in the Sussex Stakes.

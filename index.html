Green Forest was retired from racing to become a breeding stallion at Jonabell Farm. Despite a promising start to his stud career, he was not a commercial success in USA.By far his most successful offspring was the filly Forest Flower, from his first crop of foals, whose wins included the Irish 1000 Guineas.
